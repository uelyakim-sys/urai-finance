## Skills in this Collection (8)

### 1. urai-hgen — 30-Expert HGEN Round Table
Run a structured adversarial debate between 30 legendary financial minds (Buffett, Dalio, Soros, Druckenmiller, Lynch and 25 more) before every investment decision. Bull camp vs. Bear camp with cross-examination. Returns a FINCON VERDICT with clear buy/sell/hold recommendation.

### 2. urai-trade-decision — Trade Decision Engine
Generates a complete investment plan with live prices, exact share counts, Limit Orders, and Stop-Loss for every position. Never trade without a plan again.

### 3. urai-daily-briefing — Daily Portfolio Briefing
Master orchestrator that runs all URai skills in the right order. Morning briefing with live data, portfolio status, risk alerts, and GEAR scores. Runs automatically at 08:30.

### 4. urai-kelly — Kelly Criterion Position Sizer
Calculates mathematically optimal position size using Kelly Criterion + Half-Kelly + Druckenmiller filter. No more guessing how much to invest.

### 5. urai-gear — GEAR Score Hunter
Scans your full watchlist and scores every ticker 1-5 using the GEAR composite signal (momentum + earnings quality + analyst sentiment + risk-adjusted return). Shows TOP 3 opportunities daily.

### 6. urai-portfolio-status — Real-Time Portfolio Analyzer
Analyzes all positions in real-time: live price vs. entry, distance to Stop-Loss, distance to T1, Druckenmiller filter. Flags what's urgent.

### 7. urai-live-data — Live Market Data Puller
Pulls live prices for all watchlist tickers. Mandatory companion for all other URai skills. Never answer market questions from memory.

### 8. urai-laws-arm — Risk Rules Engine
Loads all active Laws and ARMs from the latest URai prompt. Identifies which risk rules are active based on live market data.
